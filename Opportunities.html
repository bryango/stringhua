POSTDOCTORAL POSITIONS
 Application deadline: 2018/12/15

The String Theory group of the Yau Mathematical Sciences Center (YMSC) at Tsinghua University invites applications for several postdoctoral positions starting from fall of 2019 (or earlier) in the areas of string theory, quantum gravity, supersymmetric gauge theories, topological quantum field theories and mathematical physics (duration: 2+1 years).

YMSC is at the forefront of research exploring the intersection between physics and mathematics. Current faculty members of the String Theory group include Chi-Ming Chang, Babak Haghighat, Si Li, Hai Lin, Mauricio Romo, Wei Song, Rak-Kyeong Seong, Dan Xie, and Wenbin Yan. YMSC offers very competitive packages, including competitive salaries and benefits, and a generous travel grant to encourage collaboration and interaction with top research centers around the world.

Completed applications must contain curriculum vitae, research statement, selected reprints and/or preprints, as well as three reference letters, which can be submitted through Academic Jobs Online or sent electronically to msc-recruitment [at] math.tsinghua.edu.cn. Review of applications will start from December 15 and continue until the positions are filled.

To apply visit Academic Jobs Online.
