Wei Song
 Associate Professor, YMSC, Tsinghua University

Research interests:
string theory, quantum gravity and holography. In particular, I work on holographic dualities for spacetime which are not asymptotically Anti-de Sitter spacetimes, inlcuding the Kerr/CFT correspondence, and flat holography. My recent work inludes holographic entanglement entropy for non-AdS holography, the Sachdev-Ye-Kitaev models and integrable irrelavent deformations.
